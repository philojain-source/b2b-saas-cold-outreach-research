# Foundational reference — *Predictable Revenue* (Aaron Ross)

Not one of the ten active voices, but included for context: most modern B2B SaaS outbound
descends from the "Cold Calling 2.0" model Aaron Ross documented from his time building the
outbound team at Salesforce.

**Why it matters for this topic**
- Popularized **role specialization** — separating prospectors (SDRs) from closers (AEs) —
  which is the structural assumption behind nearly every source in `sources.md`.
- Introduced the idea of **predictable, repeatable pipeline** as a system rather than
  hero-rep effort.
- The vocabulary the ten experts riff on (SDR/BDR, sequences, "outbound engine") largely
  originates here.

**Pointers**
- Book: *Predictable Revenue* (Aaron Ross & Marylou Tyler)
- Company: https://www.predictablerevenue.com (blog + podcast continue the lineage)

Use this as the "why the game is played this way" baseline; the ten modern practitioners
are where the current *tactics* live.
