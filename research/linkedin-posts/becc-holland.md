## 1. ~2026-07-03 — Discovery questions shouldn't contain adjectives/adverbs

> If your DISCO [discovery] questions have adjectives or adverbs (e.g. "low win rates,"
> "long sales cycles," bad, good, high, ineffective)… you're trying to *sell* them, NOT
> *help* them.

*Why it matters:* leading questions that pre-load the problem betray the pitch; neutral
questions surface the prospect's real framing.
